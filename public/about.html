About Me

This is what I do.

Who am I? I'm just a husband and father, a regular dude, a software developer, and a homebrew enthusiast. Before you start thinking I'm "normal", I am also a student of the occult, an amateur paranormal investigator, and an intrepid adventurer with a penchant for all things bizzare.

This project is part of my quest to explore unknown realms, both outer and inner, and meet strange and interesting people along the way. It is my goal to provide you with entertaining content and hopefully find some useful evidence of what's behind this veil we call reality in the process.

I thank you for visiting and stay tuned for new stories of the strange and macabre wherever I find them!
